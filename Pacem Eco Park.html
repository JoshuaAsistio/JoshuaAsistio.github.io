About

Pacem Eco-Park was established by Assumption Antipolo to raise concern for the environment among students, teachers and staff, parents and visitors to the school, by providing them with an experience of the beauty of nature and the oppurtunity to reflect on the importance of ecological security to the survival of our planet. PACEM is a short acronym for Peace And Care For Earth Ministry.The young guests will be thrilled by the fluttering of butterflies that settle on hands, heads and shoulders. This park also features a mini-zoo, herbal garden, and a museum of seashells, butterflies, and moths collected form around the world.

PACEM ECO-PARK features:
  • An Eco Center
  • A Wildlife Sanctuary
  • A Butterfly Garden
  • A Butterfly Collection
  • A Shell Collection
  • A Mini-Forest
  • An Aviary
  • An Eco-Shoppe
  • A Snack Center
  • A Herbal Garden
  • An Organic Farm
  • A Green House
