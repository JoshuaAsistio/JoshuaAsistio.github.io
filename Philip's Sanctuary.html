About

Phillip's Sanctuary is an Agro-forestry farm and teambuilding resort that was established in 1989. When the property was acquired, it only had cogon grass and tree stumps all over the place. It showed the brutal neglect of the environment that was once at one time, a forested place full of wildlife.

The Eco-outdoor venue for:
