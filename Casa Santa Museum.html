About

The Casa Santa Museum is a public attraction of Jardin de Miramar, located in Antipolo City, east of Metro Manila. The museum features Casa Santa Houses, which contain collections of Santa Claus figurines and are internationally recognized. At present, it's one of the most extensive private collections of Santa Claus items in the world, with over 3,700 figures. Visitors can interact with the figurines and dolls.
