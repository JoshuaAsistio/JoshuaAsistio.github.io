About

The Antipolo Cathedral is a Roman Catholic cathedral located in Antipolo City, Rizal in the Philippines. It is also the Immaculate Conception Parish and referred by locals as "Simbahan ng Antipolo". It enshrines the Marian image of Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage (Spanish: Nuestra Se√±ora de la Paz y Buen Viaje), and is the seat of the Bishop of Antipolo. The shrine attracts millions of pilgrims annually, especially during the Pilgrimage Season which commences with the Alay Lakad from the Quiapo Church on April 30 and lasts throughout the month of Mary.
